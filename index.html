Nissan gtr vspec

GT-R V-spec was a Skyline GT-R with an optional package that was first equipped on Model R32 GT-R in February 1993. Its standard equipment included Brembobrakes and 17-inch BBS wheels. ATTESA E-TS PRO was adopted as the drive system for the all-new R33 GT-R rolled out in January 1995. GT-R V-specs cars were used a lot as a base car for Group N endurance races.
